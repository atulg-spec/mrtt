Privacy Policy

At MRT GREEN, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with utmost transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you interact with our website, mobile applications, or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data (e.g., city, region)
  • Device and browser information (IP address, operating system, type, etc.)
  • Browsing behavior through cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to our platform and services
  • To verify your identity and process registrations
  • To improve user experience and website functionality
  • To communicate with you via email, SMS, or phone
  • To deliver updates, newsletters, and promotional content (with consent)
  • To generate environmental impact reports based on campaign participation
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it under these circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers (such as payment gateways, cloud hosting, email marketing tools)
  • With government or law enforcement agencies when legally required
  • With partners, only when necessary and with appropriate data protection agreements in place

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • SSL encryption for secure communication
  • Password-protected user accounts
  • Access controls and monitoring of internal systems
  • Regular audits and vulnerability assessments

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at mrt@mrtt.org.in.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize you when you return to the site
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Track website performance and usage statistics

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. By continuing to use our site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes your consent to the updated terms.
