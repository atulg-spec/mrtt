About MRT GREEN

MRT (Mission for Re-Greening Tomorrow) is a nonprofit initiative committed to planting 100 crore trees by 2030 to build a greener, healthier India.

We are India's fastest-growing green movement, focused on restoring nature through transparency, innovation, and community participation. From rural farmers to urban donors, MRT bridges people with purpose.

With verified tree planting, impact reports, and digital tracking, MRT ensures every rupee creates real environmental change. We invite individuals, corporates, and institutions to join us in this green revolution.
